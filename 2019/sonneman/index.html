Sample 1 - EXCO Pendant Lamp
LEFT: SolidWorks model and rendering. MIDDLE: Isometric Drawing. RIGHT: Preliminary concept sketches. EXCO lamp is designed with 2 interchangeable positions. It can be collapsed and opened to expose more light into a space. It underwent many changes during ideation, starting from abstract shapes to create an in­teresting 3D form. During research I was inspired by the clean lines of timeless mod­ernism and the materials used during this period. The final design is a cube with sliding panels to expose more light or give off a dimmer glow by sliding the panels back. The engineering challenge successfully resolved was calculating the chamfered edges of each panel and cutting the 45-degree angles on the tablesaw to fit together when closed.

EXCO Lamp Final Prototype. MATERIALS: acrylic, wire, adhesive, electrical plug, CFL bulb & socket, light switch.
Sample 2 - Bella Kids' Bedside Lamp & Smart Hub



Bella is a product concept for a Kids' lamp that syncs to your smart home, designed with a flat edge on the base as a second facing position. Inspired by the natural form of portabella mushrooms. Started with ideation sketches shown in the middle, modeled in SolidWorks, 3D printed the parts and airbrushed the assembly. This working prototype had a programmed Arduino board soldered to a speaker and blue LEDs.
