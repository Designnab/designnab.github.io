NABBING high-impact visual solutions through strategic design, Natalie A. Boni turns concepts into profitable products and services. She is a U.S.-based designer who specializes in UI for mobile and web platforms, UX solutions and desktop publishing. A lifetime of multidisciplinary exploration has honed her skills across finance, B2B, and retail, enabling her to craft attractive designs that resonate. Through exceptional client relationships, she is entrusted as a department liaison for cross-functional coordination to ensure seamless delivery. Her experience in project management and leveraging agile methodologies allows for progressive outcomes through continual process improvements. 

Natalie is a Product Design BFA graduate of Parsons The New School for Design and was awarded The Arthur O. Eve Higher Education Opportunity Program full scholarship out of over 5,000 applicants. She is currently a candidate for the Master of Business Administration in Technology Management at SUNY Polytechnic Institute and has been awarded the Graduate Opportunity Scholarship.

Natalie is selecting new projects that advance innovation and drive meaningful outcomes for all stakeholders. Contact her by email at designsbynab@gmail.com or by messaging her for a phone chat.
