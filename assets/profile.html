NABBING high-impact visual solutions through strategic design, Natalie A. Boni is a Design Researcher and Product Designer with experience across tax, fintech, and intelligent automation. She uncovers user motivations, map complex financial workflows, moderate unbiased interviews, and translate patterns into product opportunities. Recent work includes foundational research for QuickBooks, designing generative-AI financial reports, and investigating conversational breakdowns in Intuit’s virtual expert platform. She brings 6+ years of multidisciplinary design experience across publishing, operations, e-commerce, and creative strategy.

Natalie is a Product Design BFA graduate of Parsons The New School for Design and was awarded The Arthur O. Eve Higher Education Opportunity Program full scholarship out of over 5,000 applicants. She is currently a candidate for the Master of Business Administration in Technology Management at SUNY Polytechnic Institute and has been awarded the Graduate Opportunity Scholarship.

Natalie is selecting new projects that advance innovation and drive meaningful outcomes for all stakeholders. Contact her by email at designsbynab@gmail.com or by messaging her for a phone chat.
