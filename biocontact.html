Natalie Bonilla is a US based designer who specializes in digital publishing, products & visual identity. She is a Product Design BFA graduate of Parsons the New School for Design, a full scholarship recipient of the highly competitive Higher Educational Opportunity Program.

Her lifetime of multimedia exploration was followed by progressive in-house design and freelancing experience in various vertical markets, e.g., art, B2B, fashion, finance, health care, education, legal, real estate, retail products, online, etc. She is 5-star Google rated, due in part to her Spanish working proficiency. 

Natalie enjoys gathering inspiration from unexpected themes and cultures to make fresh concepts. Her creative process is enhanced by constructive input from anyone.
